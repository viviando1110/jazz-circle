## Edit #1 — Winehouse Recording: Precision Fix (REVISED after rejection)

**Why it was rejected:** Amy Winehouse DID record 'Round Midnight. It was a
B-side to her 2004 single "Take the Box" (Frank era), documented on Discogs
(release r12540462, track 2.07 on bonus disc). Our deletion request was wrong.

**What is still imprecise:** The article says "B-side to her debut album Frank."
The recording was actually a B-side to the *single* "Take the Box", not a
direct B-side to the album. Grok acknowledged this is "minor rephrasing."

**Revision strategy:** Instead of deleting, correct only the release attribution.
Small, precise, uncontroversial.

### Replacement Text

### Reason for Submission Notes

```
The recording is real and well-documented (Discogs r12540462). The minor
imprecision is the release context: the B-side was attached to the single
"Take the Box" (released January 12, 2004), not to the album Frank directly.
This edit corrects only the release attribution without disputing the substance.
```

The A section's harmonic identity centers on the Cm7b5 (half-diminished)
